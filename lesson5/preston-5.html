Farm Life

By: Napoleon Dynamite

Like much of Southeast Idaho, the small town of Preston is surrounded by farms that produce a variety of crops. Potatoes, wheat, barley, hay, beets, sugarbeets, and mustard can be seen growing in various places in the summer time. However, this is Southeast Idaho, so expect to see a lot of potato and wheat fields. As the nation and world grows, farming continues to improve and provide innovative responses to meet the growing food demand. So the next time you see a farmer, take a moment to pause and think about how much work is involved in transforming a tiny seed into the food that is on your plate.
