Support your Community

Come spend a morning at the Rexburg Farmers Market! Whether your wanting to enjoy some local produce, or start growing some produce of your own.
Porter Park has been in Rexburg since 1919, and offers a variety of attractions for families to relax. Come and take a ride on the Idaho Centennial Carousel, cool off at the Rexburg Splash Pad, and see local events at the Beehive Federal Credit Union.
The Rexburg Stake Tabernacle provides a look into the history of the town. Completed in 1911, this tabernacle held hundreds of gatherings for members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints until 1980. This beautiful building now serves as a civic center for the community.
