When the Map Turns Blue

By: Henry Howell

Bear Lake has always been a favorite of locals and non-locals alike. From the famous milkshakes made with locally-grown raspberries the surpisingly light blue water, Bear Lake's attractions have put a few resort towns on the map, with Fish Haven being no exception. Founded in 1864, this little town recieved it's name in an unsurprising way. Two settlers threw their nets into a mouth of a nearby creek, and pulled out almost 100 trout! Thus, the name "Fish Haven" was aptly given to the town.
